100 Doors Property Management is an early-stage real estate development venture dedicated to building a balanced portfolio in Chicago. Our investment decisions are driven by data analytics and thorough market research. However, we believe research is not always sufficient due diligence; our intuition is gained by exploring, experiencing, and connecting with the communities we invest in. In 2026, 100 Doors plans to acquire a flip-and-hold development project.

We have completed the renovation of two apartment units, transforming them into functional and attractive housing that serves as a cornerstone for future developments. Alongside active property management, we conduct comparative analyses of distressed properties to assess value potential and identify opportunities where strategic renovations can enhance market performance. Our ongoing research includes developing reports on Midwestern housing markets using the FHFA Home Price Index, with focused analyses of home value trends in the south suburbs of Chicago.

I wrote a short paper on Chicago home value trends by zip code to identify south side regions with the highest growth potential for 2026 flip-and-hold development projects. Our 2026 acquisition will add to a portfolio holding a south suburban two-flat acquired in 2024 with a $600 monthly cash flow.

Our willingness to invest in Chicago is not just driven by growth potential, but in a strong belief in the vibrancy, resilience, and might of the Chicago ecosystem.
