Noria is building a science-backed supplement for menopausal brain fog.

We believe every women deserves to grow into menopause with grace, autonomy, and knowledge. We’re creating Noria for women experiencing brain fog today, and for every generation that follows. Our goal is to give women a tool that not only supports their cognitive health, but also reduces the fear and uncertainty surrounding menopause so they can continue to feel like themselves, think clearly, and live fully. At its core, Noria exists to nourish women’s minds, supporting their vitality well into menopause.

Before the official launch, Noria will conducting a beta test in June 2026 and has plans to launch in September 2026.


Products

Nurishment for Energy, Mood, and Focus

This product uses three patented ingredients at clinical doses. This matters because during menopause Estrogen, a key hormone for memeory and attention, declines. We use ingredients studied in clinical settings and have been shown to support memory and attention.
